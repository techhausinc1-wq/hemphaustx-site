Common Questions

Concentrates FAQ

What is THCP live resin crumble?
THCP is a hemp-derived cannabinoid. "Live resin" refers to the extraction method, which preserves more of the plant's original terpene profile than standard extraction. "Crumble" describes the texture/consistency of the final product, not an additive or flavoring.
Is THCP legal?
THCP's legal status varies by state and is changing rapidly — it is not covered by the same clear federal exemption as broad-spectrum CBD. It is the retailer's sole responsibility to confirm this product is legal to sell in their jurisdiction before purchasing or reselling it. Tell us your state and we'll share what we know, but this isn't legal advice — check with your own counsel if you're unsure.
Will this show up on a drug test?
Yes, very likely. THCP is psychoactive and structurally similar to THC. Retailers should make sure their customers understand this before purchase — this is not a product like broad-spectrum CBD that stays under drug-test thresholds.
