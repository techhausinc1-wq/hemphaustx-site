Common Questions

FAQ

Do you sell direct to consumers?
No — HempHaus is a wholesale supplier. We sell to licensed retailers, distributors, and qualified business buyers only. If you're an individual looking to buy, ask your local retailer to carry HempHaus, or contact us and we can point you to a stockist near you.
How do I become a wholesale partner?
Fill out the wholesale form below with your business info and what you're interested in carrying. We typically respond within one business day with current pricing, minimum order quantities, and lead times.
Where do you ship wholesale orders?
Nationwide, to retailers and distributors in all 50 states, subject to each state's rules on hemp-derived cannabinoid products. Requirements vary more for THCP than for our CBD salve — tell us your state and we'll confirm what we can ship there.
Do you offer samples before a full order?
Yes — free to qualified buyers. Mention it in your wholesale inquiry below and we'll get samples out to you.
Product-specific questions?
Ingredient lists, usage details, drug-test info, and line-specific compliance notes live on each product's own page — see CBD Salve or THCP Concentrates.
