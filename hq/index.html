How to use HempHaus HQ
Everything you need to operate this platform. Read this first if you're new.
What Is HempHaus?

HempHaus Inc. is a Texas-based hemp product distribution company. We buy finished products from licensed wholesale suppliers and sell them to independent retail businesses. We do not manufacture — we are the supply chain between wholesale and the store shelf.

Three Primary Markets
⭐ San Antonio TX (78247) — Ivan's territory. Day 1 market.
⭐ Hollywood FL (33019) — Joe's home base. Expanding to Tampa/Miami.
⭐ Southampton PA (18966) — Third market. Opens after TX and FL are running.

The pricing model is keystone pricing: we buy at wholesale cost and sell to retailers at 2× that cost. This produces a 50–65% gross margin on everything we sell. The retailer then marks up again for their customer. Example: buy THCA flower at $5 → sell to smoke shop at $13 → keep $8 profit (61.5% margin).

The job: Find stores → introduce products → keep them reordering every 1–2 weeks. The Scout finds the stores. The Prospects tab tracks the relationships. The Sales Coach helps you say the right thing.
How the Scout Tool Works

The Scout is an AI-powered lead generation tool that searches the internet in real time to find real named businesses — complete with address, phone, and a priority score. You never build a cold list manually.

1
Pick your market. Click one of the three ⭐ quick buttons at the top (San Antonio / Hollywood FL / Southampton PA) or type any state and ZIP manually.
2
Choose business types. Smoke Shops, Vape Shops, and CBD Stores are pre-checked — your highest-margin, fastest-closing accounts. Add C-Stores for salve placement, or Wellness/Pharmacy for CBD supplements.
3
Set volume and hit Run. Standard = 20–30 leads (recommended daily). Deep Dive = 35–50 for full territory planning. Watch the log box — every live search is shown in real time.
4
Read the lead cards. Each card shows name, type, address, phone, score (1–10), and AI notes. Score 8–10 = large independent shop with high foot traffic — visit these first. Below 5 = likely a chain where you can't sell direct to an owner.
5
Generate outreach. Click Outreach on any card — the AI writes a personalized cold email, call script, SMS, or full strategy for that specific business. Uses your saved notes as context.
6
Add hot leads to Prospects. Check the box on any card, then click "Add to Prospects" in the floating bar — or click ⭐ Add on any individual card. Moves them to your active sales pipeline.
Resume Last Scan
When you reopen the app, a banner appears offering to resume your last scan. Your leads are saved automatically so you never lose a list between sessions. Click Resume to reload or Start Fresh for a new scan.
Stats Bar
The five numbers across the top update live as you work: Total Leads, Hot (score 7+), Prospects in your pipeline, Won accounts, and Overdue accounts that need follow-up. Your full pipeline health at a glance.
⭐ Prospects Tab — Your Active Pipeline

The Prospects tab is your CRM. Every account you're seriously pursuing gets its own card with everything you need to track the relationship from first contact to closed deal.

What each Prospect card contains
  • Status pipeline — New → Contacted → Follow Up → Meeting Set → Won → Pass. One click to update.
  • Decision Maker fields — Name, title, phone, email. Fill in the moment you get a real contact at the store.
  • Notes — Current supplier info, objections heard, best time to call, anything relevant.
  • Activity Log — Every call, visit, and email logged with timestamp and outcome type (Called / Left VM / Spoke with DM / Sent Quote / Site Visit / etc.).
  • Overdue badge ⏰ — Auto-flags accounts you haven't contacted in 3–10 days depending on status. Keeps warm leads from going cold without realizing it. Also reflected in the red "Overdue" stat at the top.
  • Outreach button — Generate fresh personalized outreach for any prospect at any time, with your saved notes and DM info used as context.
Hit Save on each prospect card after entering DM info or notes. Use Export CSV from the header to back up your full pipeline anytime.
How to Pitch
⛽ Gas Station / C-Store — The Salve Opener
"Hey, I'm with HempHaus — we supply hemp wellness products to independent stores in the area. This is our healing salve [hand it to them] — natural muscle and joint relief, no hemp license required to carry it. I can leave you a counter display today, you set your price, we settle weekly. No minimums, no commitment. Want to try it?"
Arrive with the display box already assembled. The visual does half the work. You're not asking for a purchase order — just asking them to let you leave a display. Lowest-risk ask in sales.
Smoke Shop — The THCA Lead
"What's your THCA situation right now?" [let them answer] "Most shops I talk to are buying out-of-state — slower delivery, higher prices, no local rep. We supply THCA flower, pre-rolls, and carts at distributor pricing, local supply, 2–3 day turnaround. No minimums to start. What would it take to try us on one product?"
The question "what's your THCA situation?" does all the work — most owners immediately tell you their current supplier's problems.
The 3 Objections
"We already have suppliers."
→ "I'm not asking you to switch — just earn one reorder. Try us on one SKU at your next restock. If quality and price aren't better, you go back to your current guy. Zero risk."
"We don't carry hemp stuff."
→ [Hold up the salve] "This isn't cannabis — it's a topical pain relief product. No license needed. Sits at the counter like a free sample — no shelf-space commitment, no inventory risk, no compliance friction. Hand the shopkeeper a tester jar; if they like it, they place the wholesale order on the spot."

THCA Flower, Pre-Rolls & Concentrates
The highest-margin product in your catalog. Your #1 smoke shop pitch. Currently the fastest-growing hemp SKU in the US.
What It Is
Hemp flower testing under 0.3% Delta-9 THC by dry weight — federally legal under the Farm Bill. Critical fact: when smoked or vaped, THCA (tetrahydrocannabinolic acid) converts to Delta-9 THC and produces psychoactive effects indistinguishable from marijuana. The consumer experience is essentially identical to cannabis. The legal classification is the difference. Every product ships with a COA (Certificate of Analysis) proving Farm Bill compliance.
Market Size & Opportunity
THCA flower is currently the #1 selling product in independent smoke shops across Texas, Florida, and the Southeast. The legal hemp flower market surpassed $2.8 billion in 2024 and is growing at 40%+ annually. Most independent shop owners are sourcing from out-of-state distributors with 5–7 day shipping and no local rep — meaning HempHaus can walk in with 2-day local supply and immediately become more valuable than their current supplier.
Who Buys It (End Consumer)
Cannabis consumers purchasing legally without a dispensary card. Adults 21–45 who want the full marijuana experience but live in a state where recreational cannabis isn't available — or prefer the legal status of hemp. Extremely high repeat rate: customers return every 1–2 weeks. This is the highest-frequency repurchase product in a smoke shop.
Where We Source It
Hemp Depot (Colorado — largest US hemp wholesale distributor), Cannaflower, Hometown Hero (Austin TX). All offer finished, COA-tested, retail-packaged product at true wholesale pricing. Minimum orders $500–$1,000. Unit cost drops significantly with volume — buying at 50+ eighths vs 10 makes a material difference.

Delta-8 THC — Gummies, Carts, Tinctures
The highest-velocity hemp product in the US. Every smoke shop and vape shop in the country carries it.
What It Is
Delta-8 THC is a naturally occurring cannabinoid derived from hemp. It produces real psychoactive effects — euphoria, relaxation, mild high — but significantly milder and smoother than Delta-9 (marijuana). Often described as "weed with the anxiety dialed out." Federally legal under the Farm Bill. Available as gummies (the most popular format), vape carts, tinctures, and pre-rolls. Delta-8 was invented and popularized by 3Chi (Indianapolis) in 2019.
Market Size & Opportunity
The US Delta-8 market is estimated at $5–$10 billion annually and was the fastest-growing consumer product category of 2021–2023. While growth has moderated as THCA has taken share in smoke shops, Delta-8 gummies remain the #1 selling hemp edible in c-stores and the #1 cart format in vape shops. The market is mature — meaning accounts are already buying it, and the pitch is displacing an existing supplier rather than introducing a new product category.
Who Buys It (End Consumer)
Adults 21–45 wanting a mild psychoactive experience without legal risk. People in states without recreational cannabis. Former marijuana users who want a legal alternative. Extremely high brand loyalty once a customer finds a product they like — near-100% repeat purchase rate. Delta-8 gummies are one of the most consistent impulse buys at a c-store checkout counter.
Where We Source It
3Chi (Indianapolis — created the Delta-8 category), Hometown Hero (Austin TX — strong Texas brand recognition), CBD Unlimited, Hemp Depot. These suppliers produce finished, packaged, COA-tested product. Minimum orders $500–$1,000. 3Chi has the strongest brand pull — some retailers specifically request it by name.

Custom Hemp Packaging
The highest margin in the catalog, automatic reorders, and zero compliance risk. This category is a subscription business.
What It Is
Custom Mylar Bags (MOQ 500): Smell-proof, heat-sealable, custom-printed for flower, gummies, and edibles. Required by any retailer bagging their own product.

Custom Labels (MOQ 100 sheets): Adhesive labels with QR codes linking to COA results. Required for any retail product to be compliant.

Child-Resistant Containers (MOQ 250): Required by law for THC products in most states. Push-and-turn and pop-top options.

Counter Display Boxes (MOQ 100): Custom-printed counter display units. Products in a display sell 3–5× faster than in a case. We use these ourselves for salve placement.
Market Size & Opportunity
The cannabis packaging market is $2.2 billion annually and growing at 22% per year. Mylar bags and child-resistant containers are consumables — they're used up every time a retailer sells a unit. A smoke shop moving 200 eighths per month goes through 200 mylar bags. Every bag sold means one reorder in 4–6 weeks. This is the only product in our catalog that guarantees a recurring order without any sales effort after the first close. Most retailers are sourcing packaging from distant suppliers with 2–3 week lead times. Local, fast, low MOQ wins every time.
Who Buys It (The Retailer)
Any smoke shop, vape shop, or dispensary that sells products under their own house brand or private label. This is far more common than most people realize — independent smoke shop owners regularly buy bulk flower and bag it under their own shop name at dramatically higher margins than buying retail-packaged product. They need mylar bags, labels, and CR containers every 4–8 weeks, reliably, without fail.
Where We Source It
Domestic packaging wholesalers and specialty hemp packaging suppliers. Standard supply chain — mylar bags, containers, and labels are manufactured at scale and widely available. Our value is: local availability, low MOQ (vs. going direct to overseas suppliers requiring 5,000-unit minimums), fast delivery, and the ability to bundle packaging with product orders into a single invoice and relationship.
